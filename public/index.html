Pantry by Clove

Pantry is an automated environment for growing culinary produce. The environment is automated to maintain a stable environment for optimal plant growth. Pantry eliminates the need for human-monitored garden maintenance, and instead employs automated aeroponics technology, leading to faster rates of growth and greater plant yield.





Automated Aeroponics

Aeroponic plant systems have been shown to increase rates of growth as well as overall crop yield. Leading to more fresh herbs and vegetables for you.


Optimized Lighting Cycles

Although plants are traditionally grown outdoor in a 24-hour light-dark cycle, growing them in a more efficient light cycle further increases growth rates.
